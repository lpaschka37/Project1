Welcome to Camp Finder

Camp Finder is an app for people who love camping and getting outdoors. Camp Finder uses information from National Parks based on the nerest city location. This application will provide users with information about state parks located within a predetermined radius from the nearest city. Includes directions and local weather for the area.
